General question

What are the Park Timings?

10:00 AM to 07.00 PM (Park Opening & Closing Time)
11 AM to 06 PM (Rides Operation Time)
Park is open all 365 days a year.

criteria for counting Adult and Child?

3.3 Ft to 4.6 Ft is considered as Child and charged as per Child Ticket Rates.
4.6 Ft and above is considered as Adult and charged as per Adult Ticket Rates.
Children below the height of 3.3 Ft are free to enter along with their parents / guardians.

Can we carry our own eatables?

No outside eatables are not allowed.

Do you accept Cards for making payment?

We accept all credit cards and debit cards except Amex.
